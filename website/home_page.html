Our vision for this desktop application is to improve the
educational experience for individuals with disabilities, fostering
an inclusive and empowering learning environment. By harnessing
the power of innovative features such as eye tracing technology, the application ensures that attention is seamlessly directed to the educational material, enhancing engagement for those who may face challenges in traditional learning settings. Emphasizing autonomy, users can effortlessly upload their own studying material and customize the application to adhere to their unique learning styles and preferences. Beyond individual progression, the application promotes a sense of community by offering a competitive edge—users can sign up and engage in friendly competition, earning prestigious badges and striving for the top ranking. The real-time assistive evaluation feature stands as a constant pillar of support throughout the entire learning journey, providing personalized assistance tailored to individual needs. Our vision is to create a tool that not only facilitates education but also empowers individuals with disabilities, allowing them to flourish in an accessible and enjoyable educational landscape.
